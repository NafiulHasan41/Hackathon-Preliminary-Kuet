Title: Spaghetti Carbonara
      Ingredient: 200g spaghetti
Ingredient: 100g pancetta
Ingredient: 2 large eggs
Ingredient: 50g grated Parmesan
Ingredient: 2 cloves garlic, minced
Ingredient: Salt and black pepper to taste
      Instruction: Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
Instruction: Fry pancetta in a pan until crispy.
Instruction: Whisk eggs and Parmesan together in a bowl.
Instruction: Drain spaghetti, reserving some cooking water.
Instruction: Mix spaghetti with pancetta and garlic in the pan.
Instruction: Remove from heat and quickly stir in egg mixture.
Instruction: Adjust consistency with reserved cooking water if needed.
Instruction: Season with salt and pepper to taste.
      Taste: Savory and creamy
      Cuisine: Italian
      PreparationTime: 30 minutes
      Reviews: Delicious! My family loved it.,So easy to make, and tastes amazing.
